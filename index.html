Who We Are

A Movement Born of Compassion

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit registered under the Central Government of India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We believe that collective action is the most powerful catalyst for social change. Through six flagship projects, we address hunger, illiteracy, gender inequality, animal welfare, environmental degradation, and unemployment — tackling root causes, not just symptoms.

Our foundation maintains the highest standards of transparency and accountability, holding 80G & 12A tax certifications, CSR-1 registration for corporate collaboration, NITI Aayog affiliation, and the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 quality certification.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Mission

From feeding the hungry to planting trees — every project is a thread in the fabric of a more just, compassionate India.

📚
Education

Project Bachpanshala

Providing quality education and a nurturing environment to underprivileged children, ensuring no child is left behind due to financial barriers. Building bright futures from the ground up.

📈 Thousands of children enrolled in free learning programs
🍚
Food & Welfare

Project Sewa

Serving humanity with compassion — providing food, clothing, and essential supplies to underprivileged communities. No one should sleep hungry in a compassionate society.

🍱 Regular food drives across multiple states
🌸
Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Soaring towards a brighter future — empowering women through skill development workshops, financial literacy programs, and entrepreneurship support to achieve true independence.

💪 Hundreds of women trained in livelihood skills
🐾
Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Empowering lives and spreading compassion beyond humanity — rescuing, protecting, and caring for stray and injured animals, because every creature deserves dignity and care.

🐕 Animal rescue drives and feeding campaigns
🌿
Environment

Project Prakriti

Plant for a better tomorrow — driving environmental conservation through tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and sustainable living campaigns for a greener India.

🌳 Thousands of trees planted across India
🎯
Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through vocational training, digital literacy, and career guidance programs — transforming youth potential into productive, dignified livelihoods.

🚀 Youth trained in high-demand skills & trades
